what is design strategy ?

Design strategy is a systematic and intentional approach to creating solutions that meet specific objectives. It involves analyzing the goals of a project or organization, understanding the needs of stakeholders and developing a plan to create solutions that are effective, efficient and sustainable 
        

The Design Thinking Process: 5 Steps Complete Guide

The design thinking process is a problem-solving 
methodology
used by designers to approach complex problems and find 
innovative solutions.
It typically involves five stages: empathize, define, 
ideate, prototype, and test
